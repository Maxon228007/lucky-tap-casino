FAQ

Everything you need to know

Do I need to book in advance?▼
For walk-ins, just come by! For large groups, events, birthdays, night gaming or VIP rooms, we recommend booking in advance.
Does my gaming time expire?▼
No. All purchased gaming time is saved in your account and never expires.
Can I bring my own mouse, keyboard or controller?▼
Yes, absolutely. All stations have USB ports ready for your own gear.
Is there an age limit?▼
No minimum age during the day. For night gaming (22:00-08:00), players under 15 need guardian authorization.
What games are available?▼
CS2, Valorant, Fortnite, Call of Duty, EA FC, League of Legends, Dota 2, Apex Legends, Rocket League, GTA V, Minecraft, Overwatch 2, Rainbow Six, PUBG, Genshin Impact, and many more. You can use your own Steam/Epic/Riot accounts or our guest accounts.
